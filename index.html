Spaghetti Carbonara

A creamy pasta dish with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl with grated cheese and black pepper.
  4. Combine the cooked pasta with the pancetta, then mix in the egg mixture.
  5. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper on top.

Tips & Tricks:

  • Use fresh eggs for a creamy texture.
  • Don't overcook the pasta to keep it al dente.

Chicken Stir-Fry

A quick and healthy stir-fry with chicken, vegetables, and soy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Chicken breasts
  • 1 Bell pepper
  • 1 Carrot
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • Garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and stir-fry the chicken until golden and cooked through.
  3. Add the sliced vegetables and garlic to the pan, stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and stir everything together until well-coated.
  5. Serve with rice or noodles.

Tips & Tricks:

  • Use a wok for the best stir-fry results.
  • Feel free to add your favorite veggies to the stir-fry.

Vegan Tacos

Flavorful vegan tacos with spiced beans, avocado, and salsa.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Can black beans
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Red onion
  • Corn tortillas
  • 1 tbsp Chili powder
  • 1 tbsp Lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Warm the tortillas in a dry pan or oven.
  2. Heat the black beans in a pan with chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Slice the avocado, tomato, and onion.
  4. Assemble the tacos by adding beans, avocado, tomato, and onion on the tortillas.
  5. Top with lime juice and serve with salsa or hot sauce.

Tips & Tricks:

  • Use fresh salsa for extra flavor.
  • Add a dollop of vegan sour cream if desired.
